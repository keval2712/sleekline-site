🧱 Materials & Products

What materials are used in Sleekline ceiling systems?
+
Sleekline uses two primary base materials for its ceiling systems: hot-dip galvanized steel and high-grade aluminum alloy. Galvanized steel offers exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and corrosion resistance, making it ideal for humid and industrial environments. Aluminum is lightweight, naturally corrosion-resistant, and is preferred for its aesthetic versatility—it accepts a wide range of powder coating and anodizing finishes. All raw materials comply with relevant Indian and international standards.
What is the difference between galvanized steel and aluminum ceiling panels?
+
Galvanized Steel is stronger and heavier, suitable for industrial or structurally demanding applications. Its zinc coating provides excellent corrosion resistance. Aluminum is lighter (often preferred for large-span suspended systems), naturally corrosion-resistant without coating, and offers more design flexibility—it can be extruded into complex profiles and accepts anodizing for premium finishes. Our team can recommend the right material based on your specific load requirements, environment, and aesthetic goals.
How durable are Sleekline products?
+
Our ceiling systems are engineered for a service life of 20+ years under normal conditions. Galvanized steel components are protected by a zinc coating that prevents rust and corrosion. Aluminum components are inherently corrosion-resistant and can be further protected with powder coating or anodizing. All finished surfaces undergo adhesion testing, salt-spray testing, and UV resistance testing before leaving our facility.
Are Sleekline sun louvers suitable for exterior use in Indian weather conditions?
+
Yes. Our sun louvers are specifically designed for India's diverse climate conditions—from the heat and humidity of coastal regions to the dryness of arid zones. They are fabricated from marine-grade aluminum alloy with PVDF or powder coating that withstands UV radiation, monsoon rains, and high-temperature cycles without fading, warping, or corroding. They are routinely installed on commercial buildings across coastal cities including Mumbai, Chennai, and Kochi.

🏆 Certification & Quality

Are Sleekline products certified?
+
Yes. Sleekline is ISO 9001:2013 certified, which is the international standard for Quality Management Systems. This certification covers our entire operation—from the sourcing of raw materials, fabrication processes, quality control inspections, to delivery and installation. It ensures that every product you receive has undergone rigorous quality checks at multiple stages of production.
What quality checks does each product undergo?
+
Every batch of products undergoes a multi-stage quality control process: (1) Incoming material inspection for grade verification, (2) In-process checks at every CNC machining step for dimensional accuracy, (3) Surface finish inspection for powder coat adhesion and uniformity, (4) Load and structural integrity testing for suspended systems, and (5) Final visual inspection before packaging and dispatch. Certificates of conformance can be provided on request.
