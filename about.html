Who We Are

Building the Future of Architectural Metal

Sleekline is a dynamic and innovative architectural metal solutions company committed to delivering world-class galvanized steel and aluminum products. We bridge the gap between engineering precision and architectural artistry.

Founded with a vision to transform India's interiors and exteriors, we have grown into a trusted partner for architects, builders, contractors, and developers across 15+ cities. Our ISO 9001:2013 certification is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality at every stage—from raw material selection to final installation.

We believe that the ceiling is the fifth façade of a building—often overlooked, yet fundamentally defining the character of a space. Sleekline exists to ensure that fifth façade is extraordinary.
